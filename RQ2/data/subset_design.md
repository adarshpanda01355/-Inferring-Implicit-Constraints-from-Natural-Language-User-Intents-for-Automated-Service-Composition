Travel subset

The travel subset is designed as follows:

Density balance

- Low: TRAVEL_001, TRAVEL_007, TRAVEL_023
- Medium: TRAVEL_002, TRAVEL_010, TRAVEL_011, TRAVEL_018
- High: TRAVEL_005, TRAVEL_006, TRAVEL_016

Categories covered via their gold annotations

- Temporal: multiple (TRAVEL_001, TRAVEL_002, TRAVEL_005, TRAVEL_006, TRAVEL_010, TRAVEL_011, TRAVEL_016, TRAVEL_018, TRAVEL_023)
- Spatial: TRAVEL_002, TRAVEL_005, TRAVEL_006, TRAVEL_016, TRAVEL_023
- Domain-default: TRAVEL_001, TRAVEL_002, TRAVEL_005, TRAVEL_006, TRAVEL_007, TRAVEL_010, TRAVEL_011, TRAVEL_016, TRAVEL_018, TRAVEL_023
- Logical: TRAVEL_005, TRAVEL_006, TRAVEL_010, TRAVEL_016, TRAVEL_018

Resolvability levels present

Implicit, Vague, and Borderline all appear across these 10 requests from the gold annotations.

Importance levels

Critical, Useful, and Optional all appear in the gold constraints for this subset.
